Condition Guide
What each grade means for raw (ungraded) cards
NM-MT
Near perfect. Razor corners, clean surface, 55/45 or better centering.
PSA 8-9 if submitted
NM
Near mint. Tiny corner wear, clean otherwise. Very light handling.
PSA 7-8
EX-MT
Light corner wear, minor surface touch. Still a sharp looking card.
PSA 6-7
EX
Moderate corner rounding, minor creases or scratches. Still presentable.
PSA 5-6
VG
Noticeable wear, rounded corners, possible creases. Heavily played.
PSA 4
G
Heavy wear, major creases, writing possible. Collectible only.

Pro Tips
📸 Shoot straight down on a dark surface in good light. Better photos = better AI reads.
💰 Always verify prices. Check eBay sold comps the day you list — market moves fast.
📋 Batch by account. Switch accounts in the banner before adding a new batch of cards.
✏️ Edit before exporting. Tap the eye icon on any card to review and fix the AI's work.
🔢 Check serial numbers. AI reads numbered cards (e.g. /50) but always double-check.
🔒 Your key is saved. Gemini key stores on device and survives closing the app.
