The Winning Formula
Contact over power. Luis Arraez hits .308 and puts almost every ball in play. Aaron Judge gets walked and pitched around. For BTS, contact rate beats power every time.
Great BTS pick checklist:
✓ Batting avg .285+
✓ Strikeout rate under 18%
✓ Facing pitcher with ERA 4.00+
✓ Playing in hitter-friendly park
✓ Good career history vs this pitcher
Double Down: Only when BOTH picks are 68%+. If either is under 65%, go single pick.
Avoid:
✗ Power hitters in pitcher's parks
✗ Anyone vs ace (ERA under 2.50)
✗ Player mid-slump (0-for-12+)
✗ Big name without checking lineup
